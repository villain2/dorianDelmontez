Welcome to the Dea

Dorian Delmontez is a quintette of young adult fantasy stories about a young boy who receives a magical book for his tenth birthday that leads him to a vibrant, colorful riot of a world that will change him forever.

Explore

Use the interactive map to see all the different cities, towns, landscapes and islands of the fantasy world known as The Dea. Exploring different areas of the Dea gain your different rewards.

Read

The story of Dorian Delmontez is rich and expansive. Get information on characters, backstories, family trees, environments and more. The more samples you read, the more characters you unlock.

Play

When you have enough characters and abilities stored up from exploring and reading, move on to our online game and use what you've learned to fend of the forces of Shallowhale and Avidity.
