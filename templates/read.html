Who are the Loche Brothers?

Mouth, Echo, and Sharp are the triplets known as The Loche Brothers. Each brother has a unique disability that's offset by a unique skill. While Mouth has poor hearing, his voice is powerful. Echo is mostly blind but possesses hearing so great that he can even hear information being transferred through a wire. Lastly, Sharp does not speak but his eyes can see the imperfections in a wall or illness in another person.

In this sample from Dorian Delmontez' 10th Birthday, The Loche Brother encounters Dorian and his sister Alicia as they emerge from the Springstream and set foot in the Dea for the first time.

Dorian's Home Life

Growing up in Baltimore, Dorian Delmontez didn't come from a wealthy family. He lived in the "suburban ghetto", a part of the outer counties of Maryland that housed lower-income families in often forgotten neighborhoods.

He didn't have everything growing up, but what he had he was happy for. His family, like many around him, was made up of a mixture of half-sisters and multiple grandparents. Read more about Dorian's home life in the chapter sample.

Losian Bearn - Home of Wayward Children

Not quite an orphanage, also not quite a school, Losian Bearn acts as something altogether different. Founded by a number of adults who saw too many abandoned children in the Dea, Losian Bearn is a community built to raise orphaned children in a free-spirited environment where learning, play and friendships are at the core of life.

It is the only such community in the Dea and while it sometimes is criticized by more "normal" neighborhoods for being a collection of the unwanted, the staff and kids who call this 100+ acre valley home are proud of who they are and where they come from.
